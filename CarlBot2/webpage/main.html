What is CarlBOT?

CarlBOT is the unofficial name for a past project that I decided to bring to light again and improve on its design. As the sole creator of this machine, I felt it was only right to make a blog issuing updates, and my personal thoughts, about the project. I am incredibly happy to share my invention with the people that I can and I consider myself very fortunate that even one person would even choose to read this, if you are one of those people then you have my most humble gradittude my friend!
